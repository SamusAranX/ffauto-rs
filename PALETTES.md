### UniformPs
![A visualization of the "UniformPs" palette](images/palettes/UniformPs.png)

### These palettes were derived by reducing all 24-bit colors to just 256 colors using…

#### UniformAseprite

…Aseprite's Octree method.

![A visualization of the "UniformAseprite" palette](images/palettes/UniformAseprite.png)

#### UniformFfmpeg

…ffmpeg's palettegen filter.

![A visualization of the "UniformFfmpeg" palette](images/palettes/UniformFfmpeg.png)

#### UniformPerceptual

…Photoshop's Perceptual method.

![A visualization of the "UniformPerceptual" palette](images/palettes/UniformPerceptual.png)

#### UniformSelective

…Photoshop's Selective method.

![A visualization of the "UniformSelective" palette](images/palettes/UniformSelective.png)
