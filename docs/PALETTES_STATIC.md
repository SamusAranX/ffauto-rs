### These palettes were derived by reducing all 24-bit colors to just 256 colors using…

#### UniformAseprite

…Aseprite's Octree method.

![A visualization of the "UniformAseprite" palette](docs/images/static_UniformAseprite.png)

#### UniformFfmpeg

…ffmpeg's palettegen filter.

![A visualization of the "UniformFfmpeg" palette](docs/images/static_UniformFfmpeg.png)

#### UniformPerceptual

…Photoshop's Perceptual method.

![A visualization of the "UniformPerceptual" palette](docs/images/static_UniformPerceptual.png)

#### UniformSelective

…Photoshop's Selective method.

![A visualization of the "UniformSelective" palette](docs/images/static_UniformSelective.png)
